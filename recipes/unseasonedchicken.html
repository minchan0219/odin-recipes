Unseasoned Chicken

DESCRIPTION:

Unseasoned chicken is a bland, tasteless piece of meat. It has a slightly rubbery texture and a mild chicken flavor. It is often described as being "dry" and "flavorless." Unseasoned chicken can be cooked in a variety of ways, but it is often cooked in a pan or on the grill. It can also be baked or poached. However, no matter how it is cooked, unseasoned chicken will always be bland and tasteless.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

STEPS:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Grease a baking dish with olive oil.
  3. Place chicken breasts in the baking dish.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until cooked through.
